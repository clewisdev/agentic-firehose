# Product strategy

Positioning, category creation, and the decisions that drive long-term competitive advantage — in the context of AI-era software products.

## Sources

- [Building OpenCode with Dax Raad](../../sources/2026-06-02-opencode-podcast-dax-raad.md) — "Get positioning right and the world just keeps handing you wins you didn't expect"; product was "good enough" for 5 months before optimization; category ownership (open source AI coding harness) beat execution speed.
- [What Happens to Platform Teams?](../../sources/2026-06-15-platform-teams-ai-era.md) — platform economics flip: the unit cost of custom build drops, so centralization must justify itself on cognitive load and operational risk, not development cost. Success can no longer be measured by feature velocity.
